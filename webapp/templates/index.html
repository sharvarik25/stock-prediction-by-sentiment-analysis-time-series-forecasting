Stock Prediction using Financial News Sentiment Analysis and Time Series Forecasting

A number of studies have shown strong correlation between financial news and stock price reaction.[1] The local media coverage and the financial news strongly predicts local trading, after controlling for earnings, investor, and newspaper characteristics.[2]

In the United States, recent volatility levels rival or surpass those last seen in October 1987 and December 2008 and, before that, in late 1929 and the early 1930s. Motivated by these observations, we consider the role of the financial news on the stock market and how much it can impact the stock market to predict the trends in the stock prices.

The stock market has fluctuated heavily in recent times. We aim to find how much impact does the financial news have on the stock market trends.

Goal

To find the current impact of Financial news on the Stock Market.

  1. Leveraging a real time data pipeline to create a web application which would let the user understand behaviour of the current and future of stock market
  2. Applying sentiment analysis on financial news to check its impact on the stock market
  3. Performing time series analysis which would forecast the stock prices of next 2 months
