Time Series Forecasting

Time series forecasting involves taking models fit on historical data and using them to predict future observations. The purpose of time series analysis is generally twofold: to understand or model the stochastic mechanisms that gives rise to an observed series and to predict or forecast the future values of a series based on the history of that series.

For time series forecasting we obtained the data from Redshift using Glue ETL pipeline to export it on S3 bucket in CSV format.

We used Amazon Forecast to train our ARIMA model using Date and Closing price of stocks for the period of March 2018- March 2020 to predict stock prices of next 2 months.
