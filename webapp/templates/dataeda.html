Exploratory Data Analysis

We have collected data from two different sources.

  1. Alpha Vantage

    2. Data source link: https://www.alphavantage.co

    Data consists of daily stock prices for the period of March 2018 - March 2020 from 6 different sectors with 3 companies from each sector.

    The data extracted is stored in json and csv formats along with its metadata.

  2. Wall Street Journal

    3. Data source link: https://www.wsj.com/

    Scraped articles on Wall Street Journal published for the period of March 2018 - March 2020
