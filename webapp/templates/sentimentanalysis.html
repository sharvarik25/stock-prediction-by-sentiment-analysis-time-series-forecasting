Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis (also known as opinion mining or emotion AI) refers to the use of natural language processing, text analysis, computational linguistics, and biometrics to systematically identify, extract, quantify, and study affective states and subjective information.

We scraped the content of different articles from Wall Street Journal for the period of March 2018-March 2020. The scraped content was stored in json format along with its metadata. We then used Amazon Comprehend to calculate the sentiment scores of the content and realized that the headline of the content also gives nearly the same sentiment score.

So to optimize the pipeline we switch to scraping the headline of the content rather than the whole content as it was time-consuming. We generated a list of keywords for every company considered to filter and sort the headline of the articles into each company and then perform sentiment analysis for those headlines only to further optimize our process.
